Progress Countdown

Great for counting down from the start to the end of summer break, quarantine, holidays, birthdays, that one assignment that you procrastinated on, etc. The countdown includes a progress bar to show the progress of an event for start to finish allowing for easy time management. The coronavirus background adds an interesting aesthetic and is a great way to remind us why we have to stay home (more backgrounds coming soon).
